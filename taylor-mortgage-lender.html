Home Prices in Taylor

Median home prices in Taylor range from roughly $250,000 to $420,000. New builds in master-planned communities push toward the higher end, while older homes near downtown and in established neighborhoods offer strong value below $300,000. Taylor remains one of the most affordable options in the Austin metro — but the Samsung effect means prices are moving. Buyers who act now are positioned well for appreciation.

Schools, Commute & Local Context

Taylor ISD serves the city and is expanding to meet population growth driven by Samsung and related suppliers. Taylor High School is the primary 9–12 campus. Commute times: ~15 min to Samsung's fab campus on SH-130, ~45–55 min to Austin via US-79 and SH-130 (off-peak), ~20 min to Georgetown, ~30 min to Round Rock. Taylor sits in Williamson County — property taxes and appraisals run through WCAD. The city's proximity to SH-130 (Texas 130 toll) gives commuters a viable bypass around Austin traffic entirely. For buyers commuting to north Austin tech campuses or working remotely, Taylor's price-to-space ratio is among the best in the metro.

Loan Options for Taylor Buyers

Conventional Loans

The most popular choice for Taylor buyers with solid credit. As little as 3% down with competitive rates. The 2026 conforming loan limit is $832,750 — well above the typical Taylor purchase price, keeping your options wide open.

FHA Loans

Ideal for first-time buyers or those with limited credit history. 3.5% down with a 580+ credit score. FHA's flexible guidelines pair perfectly with Taylor's affordable price points to make homeownership accessible.

VA Loans

Zero down payment and no PMI for eligible veterans and active duty military. Taylor's affordability combined with VA's zero-down benefit makes this one of the strongest buying opportunities in the Austin metro.

Down Payment Assistance

Texas programs like TSAHC and TDHCA offer grants and forgivable second loans to eligible buyers. Taylor's price points and income thresholds mean many buyers qualify for DPA. Adam can check your eligibility in minutes.
