With Adam Styer at Mortgage Solutions LP (NMLS #513013), most Austin buyers receive a full pre-approval letter within one business day. You submit basic information, Adam reviews your file personally — no bots, no call center. Get started here or call (512) 956-6010.

A mortgage broker shops your loan across 40+ wholesale lenders to find the best rate and terms for your specific situation. Banks can only offer their own products. As an independent broker, Adam Styer works for you — not a single institution — which typically means lower rates and more options, especially for complex scenarios.

Austin borrowers have access to Conventional loans (3–20% down), FHA loans (3.5% down), VA loans (0% down for veterans), USDA loans (0% down in eligible areas), Jumbo loans for homes above $832,750, and DSCR investor loans. Adam compares programs across 40+ lenders to find the right fit.

There is no out-of-pocket cost to work with Adam Styer. Mortgage brokers are compensated by the lender at closing — the same way a bank loan officer is paid. You get access to more lenders and better rates at no additional cost to you.

Refinancing makes sense when you can lower your rate by at least 0.5–1% and plan to stay in your home long enough to recover closing costs. It also makes sense to access equity via cash-out, eliminate PMI, or shorten your term. Adam Styer can run a free breakeven analysis — get a refi quote here.
