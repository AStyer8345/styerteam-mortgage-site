**Cash-Out Refinance in Austin — When Does It Actually Make Sense?**

Austin homeowners are sitting on a lot of equity right now.

I get asked every week: "Should I do a cash-out refinance?"

The honest answer: it depends on the math.

Here's when I tell clients to go for it:
- Consolidating high-interest debt (22% credit cards -> 7% mortgage)
- Funding home improvements that add real value
- Accessing capital for investment at a lower cost than alternatives

And when I say wait:
- Your current rate is under 4% (don't give that up)
- You're buying depreciating assets
- You're selling within 2 years

Texas has its own rules too — 80% LTV max, 12-month cooling period, attorney required at closing.
