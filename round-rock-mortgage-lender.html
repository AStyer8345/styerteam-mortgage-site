Loan Options for Round Rock Buyers

Conventional Loans

The most popular choice for Round Rock buyers with solid credit and stable income. As little as 3% down, competitive rates, and fast closings. The 2026 conforming limit is $832,750 — covering most Round Rock homes.

FHA Loans

Perfect for first-time buyers or those rebuilding credit. 3.5% down with a 580+ credit score. Higher debt-to-income allowances make this the go-to option when income is good but credit history is short.

VA Loans

Zero down payment, no PMI, and the lowest rates available for eligible veterans and active duty military. Round Rock's proximity to Fort Cavazos makes VA financing a common and excellent option here.

Down Payment Assistance

Texas programs like TSAHC and TDHCA offer grants and forgivable second loans to eligible buyers. Income limits apply, but many Round Rock buyers qualify. Ask Adam which DPA programs are available to you.
