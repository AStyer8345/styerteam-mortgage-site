Privacy Policy

Straight answer: if you reach out, apply, or schedule time with Adam, we use your information to help you with your mortgage. We do not sell your personal information.

Effective date: April 12, 2026

This website is operated for Adam Styer, Mortgage Solutions LP, NMLS #513013. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how to reach us if you want something corrected or removed.

What We Collect

We may collect the information you submit directly, including your name, email address, phone number, loan goal, message details, and any information you choose to provide in forms or applications.

We may also collect technical information such as pages viewed, referral source, campaign tracking details, device/browser data, and interactions with calls-to-action like application clicks, phone clicks, and scheduling links.

How We Use It

We use your information to respond to questions, provide mortgage guidance, send requested resources, follow up on loan interest, improve the website, and measure which marketing channels are actually producing qualified conversations.

If you explicitly consent, we may contact you by phone, email, or text about mortgage options, pre-approval, refinancing, and related next steps. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Who We Share It With

We may share information with service providers that help run the business and website, including CRM systems, email platforms, scheduling tools, analytics providers, secure application software, and loan-processing partners when needed to serve your request.

We may also disclose information when required by law, regulation, licensing rules, court order, or to protect against fraud, misuse, or security issues.

Cookies And Tracking

This site uses analytics and tag management tools to understand traffic, content performance, and lead activity. Those tools may place cookies or similar tracking technologies in your browser. You can limit cookies through your browser settings, though some site functions may work less smoothly.

Third-Party Links

This site links to third-party websites and services, including the loan application portal and Calendly. Once you leave this site, their privacy practices and terms apply instead of ours.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. No website or internet transmission is guaranteed to be perfectly secure, so please do not send sensitive financial documents through unsecured channels unless we specifically direct you to a secure system.

Your Choices

You can ask us to update, correct, or delete the contact information you submitted through this site, subject to any legal or business recordkeeping requirements. You can also opt out of marketing emails or texts using the unsubscribe or STOP instructions provided in those messages.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or want to request a change to your information, contact Adam directly at adam@thestyerteam.com or (512) 956-6010.
