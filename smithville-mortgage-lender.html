Loan Options for Smithville Buyers

Smithville's affordable market opens up loan programs that aren't available in higher-priced Austin suburbs.

USDA Loans

Zero down payment for income-eligible buyers. Most Smithville addresses qualify. Lower guarantee fee than FHA's mortgage insurance premium. No monthly PMI equivalent after the upfront fee. This is the go-to program for Smithville — I check eligibility for every buyer.

Best Value in Smithville

FHA Loans

3.5% down with credit scores as low as 580. More flexible DTI ratios than conventional. Strong option for first-time buyers who don't meet USDA income limits or whose property doesn't fall in a USDA-eligible zone. At $250K, that's roughly $8,750 down.

Conventional Loans

3-20% down, 620+ credit score. No upfront mortgage insurance. PMI drops automatically at 80% LTV. Best for buyers with solid credit and some savings who want to avoid the ongoing costs of FHA's MIP. Competitive rates at Smithville's price points.

Down Payment Assistance

TSAHC and TDHCA programs offer 3-5% in DPA for qualifying Texas buyers. Can be layered with FHA or conventional loans to reduce your out-of-pocket costs even further. At Smithville's price points, many buyers qualify for these programs.
