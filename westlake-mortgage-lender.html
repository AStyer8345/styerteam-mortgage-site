Loan Options for Westlake Hills Buyers

Most Westlake Hills purchases require jumbo financing. Here's what I offer and where each product fits.

Jumbo Loans

For loan amounts above $806,500. Competitive rates, 10–20% down options, available up to $3M+. Strong credit and reserves required. Best for salaried W-2 buyers with straightforward income.

Most Common in Westlake

Portfolio Loans

Held by the lender on their books — more flexible underwriting. Ideal for self-employed borrowers, business owners, or those with complex asset structures. Bank statement programs available.

Conventional (Conforming)

For loan amounts up to $806,500. If you're putting enough down to stay under the limit, conventional pricing is strong. Works for smaller homes or large down payments on higher-priced properties.

Asset Depletion / DSCR

Qualify based on liquid assets rather than earned income. Useful for retirees, investors, or buyers with significant investment portfolios but minimal W-2 income. Investment property financing also available.
