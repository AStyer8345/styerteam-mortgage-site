Loan Options for New Braunfels Buyers

Conventional Loans

The most common choice for New Braunfels buyers with good credit and stable income. As little as 3% down with competitive rates. The 2026 conforming loan limit is $832,750 — well above the typical New Braunfels purchase price.

FHA Loans

A strong option for first-time buyers or those with limited credit history. 3.5% down with a 580+ credit score. FHA's flexible qualifying guidelines make homeownership accessible at New Braunfels price points.

VA Loans

Zero down payment and no PMI for eligible veterans, active duty, and surviving spouses. New Braunfels' proximity to Joint Base San Antonio and Randolph AFB makes VA financing a popular and well-deserved option.

USDA Loans

Zero down payment for eligible buyers in qualifying rural areas of Comal County. Several outlying New Braunfels neighborhoods qualify. Adam can check any address against the current USDA eligibility map before you make an offer.
