Four ways to refinance

Rate-and-Term

Lower your payment or shorten your term. Most straightforward refi — replace your existing loan with a better rate, same balance.

Cash-Out

Access your equity for any purpose — renovations, debt consolidation, investment property. Conventional allows up to 80% LTV.

FHA Streamline

Minimal docs, fast close for existing FHA loans. No appraisal required in most cases. Typically closes in 15–21 days.

VA IRRRL

Fastest, cheapest refi available for veterans. No appraisal, no income verification, minimal paperwork. For existing VA loans only.

⚠️ Texas Cash-Out Rules (Section 50(a)(6))

Texas has unique constitutional rules for cash-out refinances: 80% LTV maximum, 12-day mandatory waiting period, 3-day right of rescission after closing, and additional closing requirements. These rules protect homeowners but require a Texas-experienced lender. We know them cold — we'll walk you through every step.
