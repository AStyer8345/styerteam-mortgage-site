Loan Options for Kyle Buyers

Conventional Loans

The most popular choice for Kyle buyers with solid credit and stable income. As little as 3% down, competitive rates, and fast closings. With most Kyle homes well under the $832,750 conforming limit, conventional financing is straightforward and affordable.

FHA Loans

Ideal for first-time buyers or those with shorter credit histories. 3.5% down with a 580+ credit score. Higher debt-to-income allowances make FHA the go-to option for Kyle buyers whose income is growing faster than their savings.

USDA Loans

Parts of Hays County — including select Kyle addresses — qualify for USDA Rural Development loans, which require zero down payment and no PMI. Adam Styer will verify your specific property's eligibility before you make an offer.

Down Payment Assistance

Texas programs like TSAHC and TDHCA offer grants and forgivable second loans to eligible buyers. Kyle's price range puts many buyers squarely within income and purchase price limits. Ask Adam which DPA programs fit your situation.
