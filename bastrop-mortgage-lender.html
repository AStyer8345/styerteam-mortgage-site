Loan Options for Bastrop Buyers

Conventional Loans

A strong choice for Bastrop buyers with good credit. As little as 3% down with competitive rates. The 2026 conforming loan limit is $832,750 — covering virtually all Bastrop homes with room to spare.

FHA Loans

Great for first-time buyers or those rebuilding credit. 3.5% down with a 580+ credit score. FHA's flexible qualifying guidelines pair well with Bastrop's affordable price points.

USDA Loans

Zero down payment for eligible buyers in qualifying rural areas — and many Bastrop County properties qualify. This is one of the best-kept secrets in Bastrop home financing. Adam can verify any property address against the current USDA eligibility map.

Construction Loans

Building on acreage in Bastrop County? A one-time close construction loan lets you finance the land and building costs in a single loan with one closing. Perfect for buyers who want to build their dream home in the Lost Pines.
