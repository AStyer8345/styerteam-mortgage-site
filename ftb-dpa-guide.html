Programs Covered in the Guide

The guide explains each program in plain English — no jargon, no fine print buried at the bottom.

TSAHC

Home Sweet Texas

Up to 5% of the purchase price as a true grant — no repayment, no second mortgage. Eligible households earning up to $167,250 in Travis County.

TDHCA

My First Texas Home

Up to 5% as a deferred second mortgage at 0% interest. No monthly payment — deferred until you sell or refinance.

City of Austin

Austin DPA Program

Up to $40,000 as a forgivable loan for income-restricted buyers. Most restrictive income limits — but the largest assistance amount available.

Program availability and limits subject to change. Verify current terms at program websites before applying. Eligible buyers may receive up to program maximums — actual amounts depend on purchase price and qualifying criteria.
