What Loan Programs Are Available for Buda TX Home Buyers?

Most Buda buyers have more options than they realize. Here's a quick breakdown of what fits where.

Conventional Loans

Best for buyers with strong credit (680+) and at least 5–10% down. No upfront mortgage insurance. PMI drops off automatically at 80% LTV. Strong rates for qualified buyers in Buda's $350K–$470K range.

Most Common

FHA Loans

3.5% down with credit scores as low as 580. Great for first-time buyers in Garlic Creek or Sunfield who need more flexibility. Monthly MIP required, but lower barrier to entry than conventional.

VA Loans

Zero down for veterans and active-duty service members. No PMI, competitive rates. If you've served, this is almost always the best option regardless of down payment savings. Strong fit for the I-35 military commuter corridor.

Down Payment Assistance

TSAHC and TDHCA programs offer grants and forgivable second liens for qualifying Buda buyers. Income and purchase price limits apply, but many first-time Garlic Creek and Sunfield buyers qualify. I check eligibility for every buyer who asks.
