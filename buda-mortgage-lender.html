Loan Options for Buda Home Buyers

Most Buda buyers have more options than they realize. Here's a quick breakdown of what fits where.

Conventional Loans

Best for buyers with strong credit (680+) and at least 5–10% down. No upfront mortgage insurance. PMI drops off automatically at 80% LTV. Strong rates for qualified buyers in Buda's $300K–$440K range.

Most Common

FHA Loans

3.5% down with credit scores as low as 580. Great for first-time buyers in Garlic Creek or Sunfield who need more flexibility. Monthly MIP required, but lower barrier to entry than conventional.

USDA Loans

Zero down payment for eligible Buda addresses and income levels. Hays County has broad USDA eligibility. Underutilized and often the best deal available — I check every Buda buyer for eligibility.

VA Loans

Zero down for veterans and active-duty service members. No PMI, competitive rates. If you've served, this is almost always the best option regardless of down payment savings.
